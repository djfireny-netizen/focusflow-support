FocusFlow Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to FocusFlow ("we," "our," or "us"). We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Focus Data: Focus duration, categories, timestamps, and usage data
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version (for app optimization)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Statistics: Feature usage frequency, session duration (local analysis only)
  • Health Data: With your authorization, we sync focus time to Apple Health's "Mindful Minutes"

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and optimize focus timer services
  • Generate focus statistics and reports
  • Unlock achievements and rewards
  • Sync to Apple Health (with authorization)
  • Improve user experience and app performance

4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 Local Storage

All focus data is stored locally on your device only. We do not upload your personal data to any servers.

4.2 iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is synchronized across your devices through Apple's encrypted services. We cannot access this data.

4.3 Security Measures

  • Apple's secure storage mechanisms
  • No personal data collection during network transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates

5. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple Health: For syncing focus data (requires your authorization)
  • StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases (follows Apple's privacy policy)
  • iCloud: For data synchronization (optional, follows Apple's privacy policy)

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection regulations, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: View the data we hold about you
  • Right to Delete: Clear all data in Settings
  • Right to Export: Export your focus data (coming soon)
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Revoke HealthKit authorization at any time

7. Children's Privacy

FocusFlow is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through in-app notifications.

9. Data Retention

Your data will be retained until you uninstall the app or manually clear your data.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: fireny@live.com
  • GitHub: https://github.com/djfireny-netizen/FocusFlow-iOS

We will respond to your inquiry within 30 days.
