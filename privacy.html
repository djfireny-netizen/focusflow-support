隐私政策

FocusFlow Go（FocusFlow - 深度专注）  |  最后更新：2026 年 4 月 8 日

欢迎使用 FocusFlow Go（以下简称"本应用"）。我们非常重视您的隐私。本隐私政策旨在说明我们如何收集、使用和保护您的个人信息。

一、我们收集的信息

本应用主要在本地设备上运行，我们尽可能减少对用户数据的收集。以下是我们可能涉及的数据类型：

  • 专注与番茄钟数据：您的专注时长、番茄钟记录、成就数据等均存储在您的本地设备上。
  • Apple 健康数据：经您明确授权后，本应用会读取或写入 Apple 健康（HealthKit）中的正念时间数据。我们不会将健康数据上传到任何外部服务器。
  • 订阅与购买信息：会员订阅和买断通过 Apple 的 App 内购买系统处理，相关交易信息由 Apple 管理，我们不直接收集您的支付信息。

二、我们如何使用信息

  • 提供和改进专注计时、白噪音播放、成就系统等核心功能。
  • 在您授权的情况下，将专注数据同步到 Apple 健康。
  • 验证您的会员订阅状态。

三、数据存储与安全

您的专注数据和使用偏好存储在您的本地设备及 iCloud（如开启）中。我们不运营独立的用户数据服务器。我们采用合理的技术措施保护您的数据安全。

四、第三方服务

  • Apple HealthKit：仅在您授权后访问，数据不会被共享给第三方。
  • Apple App 内购买：支付由 Apple 处理，受 Apple 隐私政策约束。

五、儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下的儿童，我们不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。

六、隐私政策的变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。更新后的版本将在本页面发布，建议您定期查看。

Privacy Policy

FocusFlow Go  |  Last updated: April 8, 2026

Welcome to FocusFlow Go (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

The App primarily operates on your local device, and we minimize data collection. The following are the types of data that may be involved:

  • Focus & Pomodoro Data: Your focus duration, Pomodoro records, and achievement data are stored locally on your device.
  • Apple Health Data: With your explicit permission, the App may read or write Mindful Minutes data in Apple Health (HealthKit). We do not upload health data to any external server.
  • Subscription & Purchase Information: Membership subscriptions and lifetime purchases are processed through Apple's In-App Purchase system. Transaction information is managed by Apple, and we do not directly collect your payment details.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve core features such as focus timing, white noise playback, and the achievement system.
  • To sync focus data to Apple Health with your authorization.
  • To verify your membership subscription status.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your focus data and preferences are stored on your local device and in iCloud (if enabled). We do not operate independent user data servers. We employ reasonable technical measures to protect your data.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Apple HealthKit: Accessed only with your authorization. Data is not shared with third parties.
  • Apple In-App Purchases: Payments are processed by Apple and are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page, and we recommend checking periodically.
