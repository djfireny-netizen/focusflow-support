Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1: How do I start a focus session?

Open the app, select your focus duration and category, then tap the "Start Focus" button. You can pause or stop at any time.

Q2: How do I change the white noise?

On the focus page, tap the white noise icon at the bottom and select your preferred sound (rain, ocean, forest, etc.).

Q3: Will my focus data be lost?

No. All data is securely stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, your data will also be automatically synced across your devices.

Q4: How do I view focus statistics?

Tap the "Stats" tab in the bottom navigation bar to view your focus data for today, this week, this month, and all time.

Q5: How do I get a refund for Premium?

App Store subscriptions are managed by Apple. On your iPhone: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → FocusFlow → Cancel Subscription. You can continue using Premium until the end of your billing period.

Q6: How do I clear all my data?

Settings → Data Management → Clear Data. Note: This action cannot be undone!
